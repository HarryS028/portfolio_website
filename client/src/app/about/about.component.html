About me

Hi, I’m Harry, a self-taught programmer and aspiring software developer, with a master’s degree in mathematics.

Aside from using C to implement algorithms during my degree, my programming journey really began in 2019, when I was inspired to learn Python to explore ideas in the field of machine learning. I quickly grew to love the language, finding that I could also use it to create web scrapers to collect financial data for my hobby stock trading strategies.

It soon became apparent that these skills would also be useful to my employer, and after volunteering my skills to help colleagues, programming became part of my full-time role. I currently work as a finance & companies editor for a business intelligence company covering the global water industry; my role now includes programming web scrapers and automating data collection.

Currently I am looking to gain experience as a software developer, while building a foundation for a long-term programming career. My areas of particular interest include blockchain, web, and AI development.
